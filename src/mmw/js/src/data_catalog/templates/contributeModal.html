Contribute Your Data

Add your own datasets to these data repositories to share them with a wide audience, including by searches performed in Monitor My Watershed.

WikiWatershed Data Sharing Portal

Share real-time sensor data or aquatic macroinvertebrate data using the easy-to-use WikiWatershed Data Sharing Portal for do-it-yourself environmental science and monitoring communities. Learn these protocols at https://www.envirodiy.org and https://leafpacknetwork.org.

Contribute

HydroShare

Share any type of dataset, using the HydroShare online collaboration environment for sharing data, models, and code. You can select different levels of public permissions, similar to Google Docs, and even publish your datasets with a Digital Object Identifier.
